Introducing PrintMatrix Label Maker for Mac

PrintMatrix is an advanced label designer with support for mail merge, data import, automation tags and bulk label generations.

Download PrintMatrix from the Mac App Store:
https://apps.apple.com/us/app/printmatrix/id6445904972

Feature overview:
  • Choose from hundreds of label sizes and shapes
  • Intuitive user interface that mimics the layout of actual label sheets, so you can directly type into the label location you wish to print to
  • Print single labels, partial sheets or full sheets with flexible options
  • Generate and print hundreds of labels in minutes using mail merge
  • Import data from CSV files and create labels in bulk
  • Add dynamic and personalized content to your labels using automation tags for sequential numbers, dates, timestamps, random numbers and unique identifiers
  • Print label layout grids to blank sheets for easy alignment
  • Save your labels so you can edit and reprint them anytime

Secure and offline-friendly
Unlike other apps that require you to upload your data to third-party servers, this app runs locally on your device. Your data is never shared or processed by anyone else. You can use all the features of the app even when you are offline.
